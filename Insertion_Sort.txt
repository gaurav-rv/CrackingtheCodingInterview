How does insertion sort work?
>  It loops over all positions in the array and finds the right spot for each number. 

EG:	
>  6,5,2,7,1,3,4
>  5,6,2,7,1,3,4
>  2,5,6,7,1,3,4
>  2,5,6,7,1,3,4
>  1,2,5,6,7,3,4
>  1,2,3,5,6,7,4
>  1,2,3,4,5,6,7

 Runtime:
	Time Complexity:
		Worst case: O(n^2)
		Average Case: O(n^2)
	
	Space Complexity:
		Worst case: O(n)
